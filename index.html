About the event

The day India was formed as a republic is always a proud day for us.. This day is a special day for all of us and everyone celebrates it in his/her special way. Parades are organised, National flag is hoisted at many places, cultural events are organised across the nation. Even those at home don't miss to watch the National pride being telecast live from Rajpath.

How can a runner or a fitness enthusiast show his Pride of Nation..? By running.. !!!

We thought of organising a marathon at one place but then why to limit our National pride to a confined region. Let's make a nation-wide celebration. Let all runners across the country run and pay tribute to the Nation. We have the Republic Day Virtual Run. Run at your chosen place and at your chosen time. Sent us your run details and we send you your medal.

Run Information

Run anytime of the day and Anywhere where you want - Run wherever you are comfortable, on your favourite route, in the town you are visiting or in a nearby stadium. Run at whatever time you are comfortable at (or get the time) in the whole of 24hrs, starting from 0000 hrs till 2400 hrs (IST), on 26th January 2019. Just get on the road and pay your tribute to/salute the Republic of India.

Runners record their individual runs in any GPS based sports app. Completion is based on Honour System. Share your run details with us later.
